This repository serves as a portfolio and archive for my Printed Circuit Board (PCB) designs developed using **KiCad**. It includes everything from initial schematic capture to 3D modeling and production-ready manufacturing files.

---

## 🎯 Engineering Objectives
* **Schematic Capture:** Designing clean, hierarchical schematics with proper electrical rule checks (ERC).
* **PCB Layout:** High-speed routing, differential pairs, and optimized ground planes.
* **Component Management:** Creating custom footprints and 3D models for non-standard components.
* **Manufacturing Readiness:** Generating Gerber files, Drill files, and Bill of Materials (BOM) for fabrication (JLCPCB/PCBWay).

---

## 🏗️ Design Workflow
My design process follows industry standards for hardware development:



1. **Schematic Design:** Defining connections and selecting components.
2. **Footprint Assignment:** Linking symbols to physical footprints.
3. **Board Layout:** Defining board edges, placing components, and routing traces.
4. **Design Rule Check (DRC):** Ensuring the board meets manufacturing constraints.
5. **3D Visualization:** Verifying mechanical fit and component clearances.
